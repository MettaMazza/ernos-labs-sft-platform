# Ernos Labs designation policy

**Ernos Labs** is reserved by Maria Smith as the name and conformance
designation of this open-science movement. This policy concerns representation
and endorsement, not the public's open-license rights to the code, papers or
evidence.

## Uses that do not require permission

You may truthfully:

- identify this repository or a released paper as originating from Ernos Labs;
- cite, review, criticize or compare Ernos Labs work;
- say that a fork is based on or compatible with a named Ernos Labs release;
  and
- reproduce the name where an open license requires preservation of attribution.

These uses must not imply endorsement, official status or conformance that has
not been granted.

## Conforming community use

An independent project may describe itself as participating in Ernos Labs only
while it follows the current public constitution, exposes its full evidence and
derivation chains, preserves unfavorable results, separates proof from
measurement and accepts technical review through the published gates.

Work that bypasses those standards must describe itself as an independent fork
or derivative and must not use the Ernos Labs name as a badge of scientific
admission.

## No restriction on criticism or research

Loss or absence of the designation does not restrict lawful use, modification,
distribution, testing, falsification or criticism under the repository's open
licenses. It means only that the work is not represented as admitted or endorsed
by Ernos Labs.
