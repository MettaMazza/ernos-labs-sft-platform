# Physics external measurement custody

This directory contains immutable retrieval snapshots from official measurement,
standards and scientific-data bodies. They are external records, not premises of
the Fold derivation and not sources of candidate-law selection.

For every empirical Physics claim, a source is assigned before prediction to
one of three roles: development-only observation, withheld post-seal target, or
unfavorable control. The same row cannot serve as both development material and
a withheld target. Target contents remain outside the capability-closed
prediction package until the registered prediction and complete trace are
sealed.

The registry records retrieval-day SHA-256 identities. A future change at an
external URI creates a new snapshot identity; it never rewrites these files or
their hashes. Decimal, signed, floating and uncertainty fields in a measurement
body's record remain quoted external records. They never become SFT proof
scalars. Exact comparisons use derived dimension carriers and exact positive
ratios, with orientation held as a label rather than a negative magnitude.
