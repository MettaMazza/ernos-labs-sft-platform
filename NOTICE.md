This v3 repository is a new clean-room reconstruction created because the SFT
corpus has expanded beyond the organization and scale of its earlier builds.

Existing papers, reports, DOIs and repositories remain preserved as the
chronological scientific record. They are not marked invalid and are not
deleted. Public editions will be labelled superseded only when a corresponding
v3 derivation, replication and replacement publication are complete and Maria
Smith explicitly authorizes the coordinated update.

The intended notice for preserved publications is:

> Preserved development and publication record. A third clean-room
> reconstruction is in progress because of corpus growth and future scientific
> scale, not because the preserved work has been withdrawn. This edition will
> be superseded when its corresponding v3 reconstruction and replication are
> complete.

SFT-derived, self-hosted languages and principles. That future registration
does not reduce the evidentiary standing of v3.
