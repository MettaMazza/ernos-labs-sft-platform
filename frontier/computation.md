# Classical Computation frontier boundary

The frozen 113-claim branch inventory has no unclassified internal obligation.
The following famous unrestricted questions are deliberately outside that exact
closure and require separate registered grammars and certificates:

- unrestricted SFT Busy Beaver behavior;
- Fold P versus NP;
- arbitrary circuit lower bounds; and
- implementation or physical-performance claims.

Finite generated investigations may be exact at their stated boundary, but they
cannot be promoted to unrestricted theorems without depth-independent closure.
