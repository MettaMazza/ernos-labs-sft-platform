# Comprehensive branch-paper protocol

A branch paper is written only after its current-knowledge inventory is frozen
and every required obligation has a model-admitted engine receipt.

The paper must cover every registered derivation in dependency order and state:

- the question and exact theorem statement;
- generated candidate grammar and boundary;
- complete census and rejected alternatives;
- forced survivor, minimality and named-shape uniqueness;
- independent certificate and adverse controls;
- empirical registration, blind seal and external measurements where required;
- correspondence with established work only after the SFT derivation;
- meaning, consequences and exact limitations; and
- a complete evidence map from prose to source, receipt and artifact hashes.

The branch publication gate halts if an obligation is missing, conditional,
unclassified, frontier, unvalidated or absent from the paper evidence map.

Passing this gate establishes scientific readiness. Pushing or publishing still
requires Maria Smith's explicit authorization.
