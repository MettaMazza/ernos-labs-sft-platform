Replaced v3 publication artifacts will be preserved here. Earlier external
papers remain in their original repositories and DOIs and are represented by
the prior-work ledger.

The source of the inaugural V3 methods paper, **There Is No Nothing**, is
preserved as [`THERE_IS_NO_NOTHING_METHODS_PAPER_001.md`](THERE_IS_NO_NOTHING_METHODS_PAPER_001.md).
Its immutable published edition remains at
[`10.5281/zenodo.21514890`](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21514890). It is
superseded as the repository landing paper by the completed Foundation branch
paper, without rewriting its historical claims or publication record.
