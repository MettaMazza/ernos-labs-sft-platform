# Published Physics branch

The frozen Physics inventory, exhaustive paper and evidence release are
published at DOI [`10.5281/zenodo.21520881`](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21520881).

The branch contains 132 required and eight supplemental model-admitted claims,
35,840 generated candidates, one survivor per claim, 560 passing adverse
controls, implementation-distinct reconstruction and fourteen exact
measured-value correspondences. Its ten ordered subbranches cover measurement
and metrology; mechanics; interactions and fields; waves; thermodynamics;
physical quantum theory; matter, particles and nuclei; spacetime and
gravitation; continua and collective matter; and the Physics-to-Astronomy and
Cosmology boundary.

- preview-first 331-page PDF;

- complete evidence/source archive; and
- SHA-256 checksum ledger.
