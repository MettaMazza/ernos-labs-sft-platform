## Exact domain and central result

Information is represented by complete generated alternative support, exact
observation classes and complete retained/closed distinction ledgers. The first
Fold supplies the native distinction unit. Encoding is a provenance-retaining
total relation; information quantity is the exact support and distinction
profile; uncertainty is the class of distinctions an observation closes;
compression requires reconstructing source evidence; channel capacity is the
complete family of greatest structurally distinguishable code supports; and
correction is exact only for disjoint registered error-image families.

Probability is an exact part relation over complete deterministic support, not
a stochastic cause. Entropy is a provenance-retaining class ledger, not an
imported logarithmic scalar. Mutual and conditional information are exact joint,
marginal and restricted incidence ledgers. Information loss is the exact source
distinction family closed by non-singleton image fibres, while reversibility
requires singleton fibres or retained predecessor labels.

The final claim establishes finite quantum-information support correspondence
only. It does not claim operational phase, interference, entanglement,
measurement dynamics, gates or quantum circuits; those remain explicitly
assigned to the Quantum Computation branch.

## Review route

Run the complete repository verifier:

```text
python3 -m sft verify-all   # macOS and most Linux systems
py -m sft verify-all        # standard Windows Python launcher
```

For a single claim, begin with its `WHY_DERIVATION_CHECK.md`, then inspect its
registration, complete candidate census, elimination receipt, controls,
certificate, execution binding, independent validator and model-admitted engine
receipt. The standalone paper evidence map binds every paper section to those
artifacts and their exact hashes.
