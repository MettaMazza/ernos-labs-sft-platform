# Explicit SFT empirical method

## Scientific purpose

SFT joins derivation and experiment without allowing an experimental answer to
author the law later presented as predicting it.

Four evidence objects remain distinct:

1. mathematical proof;
2. computational proof by construction;
3. blind prediction; and
4. post-seal empirical measurement.

## Required protocol

### Development observation

State exactly which observations exposed the phenomenon or material relation.
Store their provenance. A relation derived from observation is labelled
`observational_derivation`, not direct forcing.

### Explicit relation

Publish a cold-readable proposed law containing its premises, exact generated
domain, selection or closure relation and falsification condition.

### Preregistration

Before prediction, register:

- source and dependency hashes;
- inputs and identities;
- candidate grammar and boundary;
- target identity while withholding its content from execution;
- metric definitions and evaluator identity;
- controls;
- stopping conditions; and
- exact claim scope.

### Target-inaccessible execution

The prediction runtime must be structurally incapable of reading targets,
comparison scores or post-seal measurements. Discipline or an agent promise is
not an access control.

V3 uses one portable capability policy on macOS, Windows and Linux. The
prediction language contains no filesystem, network, subprocess, clock,
environment, dynamic-import or foreign-function operation. A separate custodian
holds the target package until the prediction seal exists. This requires only
Python's standard library; it does not make the false claim that Python can
uniformly sandbox arbitrary native code on all three systems.

### Complete output and seal

Write the complete declared prediction, state trace, census and resources.
Cryptographically bind them to the registered source and inputs before target
access.

### Separate measurement

A separate evaluator verifies the seal, opens the target and computes only the
registered metrics. Measurement cannot rewrite the selector or prediction.

### Complete evidence retention

Preserve favorable, unfavorable, failed and tampered rows. Do not select a
success case, suppress an adverse observation or allow commentary to erase an
executed artifact.

## Why blind opacity does not close a law

Blindness establishes that a held-out answer did not enter execution. Repeated
accuracy can therefore establish the reliability of an opaque predictor.

Opacity still withholds the operational relation from premises to result. A
score alone does not expose the candidate domain, state admission, eliminated
alternatives, physical meaning of parameters or a machine-checkable law.

Accordingly:

- an opaque blind predictor may be a valid comparator or reliable instrument;
- its observations support a performance claim within the tested domain; but
- its accuracy alone cannot close an unstated fundamental law.

SFT law claims require blind prediction plus transparent derivation,
construction, sealing, measurement and falsification.

## Minimum empirical receipt

Every receipt records:

- run and protocol identity;
- source, dependency, input and artifact hashes;
- runtime target-access audit;
- capability-closure and separate-custody certificates;
- complete output identity;
- seal time and evaluator order;
- all registered measurements;
- resources used;
- favorable and unfavorable rows;
- tampered-control outcomes; and
- the exact boundary of the conclusion.
