# Contributing to the SFT clean-room reconstruction

## Before beginning

Read:

1. `CONSTITUTION.md`;
2. `AGENTS.md` if an automated assistant is involved;
3. `docs/CLEAN_ROOM_PROTOCOL.md`;
4. `docs/CLAIM_LIFECYCLE.md`; and
5. `docs/EMPIRICAL_METHOD.md` for natural-science work.

## Submission route

1. Open a claim registration without encoding the desired answer.
2. Name dependencies, provenance classes, grammar and exact boundary.
3. Generate the candidate domain.
4. Execute every declared alternative.
5. Supply minimality, uniqueness and adverse controls.
6. Supply source-bound receipts and independent certification.
7. For empirical claims, preregister and seal before target access.
8. Update the census without overstating status.
9. Request mechanical and scientific review.

## Review gates

The mechanical gate checks schemas, exact execution, dependencies, candidate
closure, hashes, controls and certificates.

The scientific gate checks meaning, scope, empirical protocol,
interpretation and correspondence. Specialist review cannot waive mechanical
failure, and mechanical success cannot manufacture missing empirical evidence.

## Ernos Labs designation

Contributors may identify conforming independent work as part of the open Ernos
Labs movement only while it adheres to the current public constitution and
exposes the complete evidence required for review.

official experimental conclusion. Maria Smith selects official runs, retained
conclusions and publications.

selected and `LICENSE_PENDING.md` is replaced by the approved licenses and
